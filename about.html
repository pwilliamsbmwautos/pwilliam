About me

William Paul Abresch Founder and CEO of Pwilliams.bmw.autos group was born on the twenty-first (21) of November 1955 into a German family of Peter and Elizabeth Abresch. His family moved to London, where he continued is educational journey, he did his elementary education and later went to Kingsbury high school (1968-1973) to further his education, he was a student with excellent record, he actively participated in school activites such as the debate team, soccer team and he was also appointed class representative due to his oustanding performances. After finishing from Kingsbury high school he gained admission into The college of Central London to study Business Management which he came out with academically distinction. His educational journey didn't end there. He decided to study Psycology and Strategic Mamagement at Univercity of London where he obtained a bachelor degree in Psychology and also a masters degree in Strategic Management which he graduated with high academic distinction in both disciplines.


Williams was barely sixteen (16) years of age when he lost his father, which left him with only his mother as he is the only child of his parents. Sadly in 2014, Williams lost his precious mother, also in 2013 he lost his precious wife, Rebecca who was his high school girlfriend to leukemia. Before she passed away, Williams and Rebecca had a beautiful daughter, Regina Abresch who is fortheen years of age in college. 


In March 1981, Williams began to work for Bayerische Motoren Werke (BMW) as a sales represntative and in no time was promoted to sales manager due to his great performances, he also received loads of awards and achievement. He worked in Bayerische Motoren Werke (BMW) for over twenty-five (25) years in over seven (7) major branches which made him travel all over Europe. In January 2010, Williams decided to resign from Bayerische Motoren Werke (BMW).


Few Months after he resigned from Bayerische Motoren Werke (BMW), Williams decided to start his own private automoblie dealership led to the origination of Pwilliams.bmw.autos group. Pwilliams.bmw.autos group is a private automobile dealeraship company that works under contracts and deals specifically on luxury cars, which has so far been very successful and broad. 


Pwilliams.bmw.autos is one of the best rising automotive retailers and wholesalers featuring most domestic, intenational and import franchises. Our stores serve urban and rural populations throughout the Western, Midwest United States and Europe. We are focused on providing customers with an honest and simpler buying and service experience.
